Big Basin Redwoods State Park

35 plots - Coast redwood forest

This stewardship research area includes 35 plots collected in a second-growth coast redwood forest with some remnant old growth. The site burned in the 2020 CZU Complex and plots are stratified across a burn severity gradient. In the future, local managers intend to conduct a prescribed burn on this site.
